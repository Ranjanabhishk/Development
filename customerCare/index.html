  • Forgot Password?

    How can I reset/retrieve my Password?

    To reset your password, follow these simple steps:

    Go to 'My Activity' dropdown. Click on 'Login'.

    Click on the 'Need Help' link. Select ' I don't know my password'.

    Enter any one of the following:
    Registered Email ID
    Registered Mobile number.

    On entering your registered Email ID or registered mobile number, an OTP will be sent on your registered mobile number & registered Email ID. Enter the OTP and create the new password.
